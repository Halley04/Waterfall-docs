Effective date: July 6, 2026

Passionate Mind (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the Waterfall mobile application (“Waterfall”, “the app”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Who we are

App name: Waterfall
Bundle ID: com.passionatemind.waterfall
Contact: GitHub Issues (support)

You may add a dedicated support email here before App Store / Play Store submission if required; until then, use the public docs repo Issues link above.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account and profile

  • Sign-in via Kakao (we receive a Kakao user identifier used to create your account).
  • Nickname and optional profile photo you provide in the app.

2.2 Location data

  • Precise device location (GPS and related sensors) while you use the app.
  • Background location when you grant “Always” / background permission, so Waterfall can detect when you enter or leave a focus zone and update blocking accordingly.
  • We do not sell your location data or use it for advertising.

2.3 Focus zones and sessions

  • Private waterfalls you create: name, coordinates, radius, and enabled state.
  • Focus session records: which zone you entered, start time, end time, and duration.
  • Per-zone statistics such as total focus time and streaks.

2.4 Public waterfalls and leaderboards

  • For curated public locations (e.g. libraries), if you visit and accumulate focus time, your nickname, profile photo, and total duration may appear on a leaderboard visible to other signed-in users.

2.5 App blocking configuration

  • iOS: Your selected apps to block are stored using Apple’s Screen Time / Family Controls APIs. Waterfall does not receive the contents of those apps.
  • Android: Package names of apps you choose to block, stored on your device and used by the blocking service while a focus session is active.

2.6 Technical and security data

  • Authentication tokens and account identifiers managed by our backend.
  • Firebase App Check signals to help prevent abuse of sign-in endpoints.
  • Standard server logs (e.g. IP address, request timestamps) for rate limiting and security.

3. How we use information

  • Authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • Detect geofence enter/exit events and control app blocking.
  • Store and display your waterfalls, sessions, and statistics.
  • Show public leaderboard entries where applicable.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Where data is stored and processed

We use third-party services, including:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Cloud Firestore database, Cloud Storage (profile images), and Cloud Functions (sign-in and maintenance jobs). Data may be processed in the United States and other regions where Google operates.
  • Kakao — Social login and local place search API (when you use search features).
  • Naver — Map display in the app.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy in addition to this one.

5. Data retention

  • Account and profile data are kept until you delete your account.
  • Closed focus sessions older than 90 days may be automatically deleted by our servers.
  • When you delete your account, we delete associated private data, leaderboard entries, and stored profile images as part of our cleanup process.

6. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With service providers listed above, to operate the app.
  • On public leaderboards, as described (nickname, photo, duration).
  • When required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

7. Your choices and rights

  • You can deny or revoke location permission in device settings (blocking may not work correctly).
  • You can edit your nickname and profile photo in the app.
  • You can delete private waterfalls you created.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us; this removes associated cloud data subject to backup and legal retention limits.

8. Children

Waterfall is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided us data.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted transport (HTTPS), Firebase security rules, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. International users

If you use Waterfall from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred internationally. By using the app, you consent to such transfers where permitted by law.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new version on this page and update the effective date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions about privacy: open a GitHub Issue on the Waterfall-docs repository.

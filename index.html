Waterfall

Focus zones on the map. Block distracting apps when you arrive.

Home Privacy Policy Terms of Use

How it works

  1. Create a waterfall — a focus zone on the map with a radius you choose.
  2. When you enter the zone, Waterfall can start a focus session and block apps you selected.
  3. When you leave, blocking turns off and your session is saved.

Platforms

  • iOS: Uses Apple Screen Time (Family Controls) to shield selected apps.
  • Android: Uses an accessibility-based blocking flow for selected apps.

Waterfall uses your device location to detect when you enter or leave a focus zone, including while the app is in the background.
